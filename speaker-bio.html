George Holt is a software engineering leader with more than 25 years of experience at Amazon, Microsoft, and Snowflake. His work has spanned developer tools, engineering systems, AI, and user experience, with contributions ranging from Windows releases to modern AI-powered developer platforms.

George is known for building high-trust, high-accountability teams that deliver at scale while staying healthy and sustainable. He brings a practical perspective on how culture, tooling, and leadership combine to shape great engineering outcomes. He has spoken at events including the AWS Summit and Microsoft Build, sharing insights on scaling developer experience and leading teams through change.
