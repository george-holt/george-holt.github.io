About Me

Across my career, I’ve consistently gravitated toward roles where the challenge was to fix what’sF broken, scale what works, and build healthy organizations around both. I’ve been the second leadership hire on a new Microsoft team that grew to 60 people, and I’ve also led multi-team organizations at Amazon and Microsoft. That breadth — from early formation to large-scale management — gives me a unique perspective on how to grow teams and products responsibly. What defines my work isn’t just product outcomes, but how those outcomes are achieved.

I focus on:

  • Coaching engineers and managers into leadership roles, developing a pipeline of future leaders who extend impact beyond my own span of control.
  • Driving cultural transformations — shifting teams from reactive firefighting and burnout toward proactive, sustainable engineering practices.
  • Public speaking & thought leadership: A speaker at major industry events including 2019 AWS New York Summit (18,000+ attendees) and 2011 Microsoft Build, sharing best practices in developer tools and engineering systems.
